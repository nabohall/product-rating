Final Fantasy XIV: Heavensward

Average rating:

This MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game) is one of the many, many games in the Final Fantasy line. Developed and published by Square Enix, it was released in June 2015 for the PS3, PS4, PC and Mac, although the Mac version has been pulled for now.

Set in the world of Eorzea, the player gets to create a character and choose a class, either one of the Disciples of War, or a Desciple of Magic. However, throughout the course of the game, the player my choose to play as any one of the available classes, on the same character!
